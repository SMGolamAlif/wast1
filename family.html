Sheikh Fazilatunnesa Mujib. Marital partner of Bongobondu Sheikh Mujibur Rahman.

Sheikh Fazilatunnesa Mujib (7 August 1930 - 15 August 1975) was the first First Lady of Bangladesh and the wife of the first President Sheikh Mujibur Rahman . She was killed along with her husband, three sons and two daughters-in-law on August 15, 1975.

Sheikh Hasina. Daughter of Bongobondu Sheikh Mujibur Rahman

Sheikh Hasina (born 26 September 1947) is the current Prime Minister of Bangladesh . She is the head of the ruling party of the 11th National Assembly of Bangladesh and the president of the Bangladesh Awami League . He is the longest serving Prime Minister in the history of Bangladesh.

Sheikh Kamal. Son of Sheikh Mujibur Rahman.

Sheikh Kamal (5 August 1949 - 15 August 1975) was the eldest son of Sheikh Mujibur Rahman . He was one of the organizers of the liberation war in Bangladesh. During the war he served as the ADC of Mohammad Ataul Gani Osmani . On July 14, 1985 , he married athlete Sultana Khuki . He is the founder of Dhaka Abahani Limited . He died on 15 August 1975.
